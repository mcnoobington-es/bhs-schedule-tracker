Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 12, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to BHS Bell ("we," "our," or "us"). This privacy policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when you use our website located at bhsbell.com (the "Site"). This Site is operated by two current students as a service for the Barcelona High School community.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Site does not directly collect, store, or process any personal data from users. However, third-party services we use (Netlify for hosting and GoDaddy for domain services) may collect certain technical information as described below.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

Our website does not:

  • Require user registration or accounts
  • Collect personal information such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers through forms (except when you voluntarily contact us)
  • Use analytics tools to track user behavior
  • Place advertising cookies on your device
  • Store any personal data in databases

3. Information Collected by Third Parties

3.1 Netlify (Hosting Provider)

Our website is hosted on Netlify. When you visit our Site, Netlify may automatically collect certain technical information, including:

  • IP addresses
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Referring URLs
  • Pages visited and time spent on pages
  • Date and time of visits

This information is collected through server logs and is used for website performance, security, and troubleshooting purposes. For more information about Netlify's data practices, please review their privacy policy at https://www.netlify.com/privacy/.

3.2 GoDaddy (Domain Registrar)

Our domain name is registered through GoDaddy. GoDaddy may collect DNS query information and other technical data related to domain resolution. For more information about GoDaddy's privacy practices, please visit https://www.godaddy.com/legal/agreements/privacy-policy.

4. Cookies

Our website uses a small cookie to remember your consent choice regarding this cookie policy. This cookie is stored locally on your device and contains only information about whether you have accepted our cookie notice. It does not track your browsing activity or contain any personal information.

Netlify may also use essential cookies required for the proper functioning and security of the website. These are technical cookies necessary for delivering the website content.

You can control and/or delete cookies as you wish through your browser settings. However, please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of this and many other websites.

5. Contact Information

If you choose to contact us via email or WhatsApp (as provided on our Contact page), you are voluntarily providing us with your contact information. We will only use this information to respond to your inquiry and will not share it with third parties or use it for any other purpose without your consent.

6. External Links

Our Site contains links to external websites, including:

  • Barcelona High School official website
  • Other third-party sites

We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these external sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites you visit.

7. Children's Privacy

This website is intended for use by students, parents, and staff of Barcelona High School. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided personal information to us, please contact us so we can delete such information.

8. Your Rights Under GDPR (European Users)

As we are based in Spain and serve users in the European Union, you have certain rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right to Access: You can request information about what data (if any) we hold about you
  • Right to Rectification: You can request correction of inaccurate information
  • Right to Erasure: You can request deletion of your data
  • Right to Restrict Processing: You can request limitation on how your data is used
  • Right to Data Portability: You can request your data in a portable format
  • Right to Object: You can object to certain types of processing

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided on our Contact page.

9. Data Security

While we do not collect personal data directly, we take reasonable measures to ensure the security of our website. Our hosting provider, Netlify, implements industry-standard security measures including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all connections
  • DDoS protection
  • Regular security updates and patches
